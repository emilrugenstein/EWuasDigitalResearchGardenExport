Background Maasai Knowledge

Maasai age groups

  1. Young ones
    1. Inkipaata -> circumcision as transition to next group (newly circumcised)
      1. traditionally with around 20 years, but nowadays often done earlier
  2. Newly circumcised
    1. Shaving ceremony as transition to next group
      1. nowadays boys can decide if the go back to school afterwards or the become a real Morang
  3. Warriors (Morran)
    1. Climax of Maasai live
    2. The often grow their hair very long
    3. Another very big ceremony (called "Eunoto") which ends the Morran-Ship
  4. Young elders
    1. Last ceremony is the "Olngesher" where the younger left hand group and the right hand group are joined together as one cohort
  5. Elders
